Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom. Click picks the nearest vertex or edge, the exact triangle under the pointer in Face mode, or a scenery object in Scenery mode. ⌘/Ctrl-click adds more to the selection in Vertex, Edge and Face modes, so Paint face(s)/Delete edge(s)/dragging a vertex act on everything selected at once. Drag a selection's gold marker to move it — scenery sticks to the terrain under the pointer. ⌫ removes the selected edge(s) or object, ⌘Z undoes, esc clears or cancels a split. Lesson stones and checkpoint torii are curriculum-owned and stay put; the summit gate shows as reference.
